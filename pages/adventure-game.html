Murders in the Rue Morgue

Welcome! In this game you are trying to solved the mysterious murders in the Rue Morgue. You are a detective, very much the Sherlock Holmes-type. You are accompanied by your friend, who will occationally aid you when your lost.

Here are the basic rules:

  • Use "Go to", "look at", "inspect" and "talk to" to perform actions on the world's items/people or move around the world.
  • Collect all the necessary clues to solved the mystery. You collect them by perfoming certain actions on the items that hold the clues.
  • Once you're ready to solve the mystery, type "Solve Mystery"
  • Beware! You get 2 strikes on trying to solve the mystery before gathering all the necessary clues. At the third one, you lose!

Once you're ready to start the game, type "Start" in the box below.

Good luck!
